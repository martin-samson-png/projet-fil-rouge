R.E.P.O. is an online cooperative horror game developed by Semiwork Studio. Playable with up to six players, it immerses you in terrifying environments where you must locate, handle, and extract valuable objects while avoiding terrifying monsters. Currently in early access on Steam, R.E.P.O. invites players to contribute to its development by providing feedback and suggestions. The game is available in both French and English.
